Our vocation

What we do for the indigenous community

Raising Indigenous Self-awareness

Sensitizing the indigenous population to understand their rights, existence, and history and to argue them to be responsible for themselves by providing different training that will encourage them to bring their attention to their growth and development for a better future.

Providing Educational Support

Encouraging, providing and distributing the necessary school materials and fees, educational guidance, counseling, and follow-up for indigenous students in Kirundo province, both new beginners and school dropouts, to advance their education.

Promoting indigenous social inclusion

Capacity building of Indigenous people toward their social integration in a Burundian cultural context aiming to advocate the establishment of a respectful space for them to harmonize, learn and interact with the rest of the community in an equal manner.

Our dedication to our community

Our Mission

Promote access to quality education for young, marginalized indigenous people (Batwa) in the Kirundo province of Burundi by reducing school dropouts through providing school materials and fees for Batwa youth, and working with local authorities to ensure that every kid in the Batwa community gets access to formal, basic education.

Learn More

Our Vision

Aspire to unlock the potential of young indigenous people by fostering self-awareness, a better understanding of their rights, and enriching their leadership skills through easy access to high education; thus creating bright leaders for a bright future.

Learn More

Our Objectives

Our objectives aim at transforming the lives of the Batwa people by reducing 40% of illiteracy in the Batwa community through providing educational opportunities by 2035, focusing on an improved understanding of human rights, access to primary & high education, means of subsistence, healthcare education, gender, environment, and culture.
